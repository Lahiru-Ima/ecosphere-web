Community Workshops at EcoSphere Urban Farm & Sanctuary

At EcoSphere Urban Farm & Sanctuary, we believe that education is key to creating a more sustainable and compassionate world. Our interactive community workshops provide hands-on learning experiences for individuals, families, and groups interested in urban farming, ethical animal care, and organic gardening.

Learn & Grow With Us

Join our interactive workshops and discover how to grow your own food, care for animals, and live sustainably.
